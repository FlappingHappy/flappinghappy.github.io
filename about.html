About the Artist and Author

Mikel Williamson is the creator of Flapping Happy and
the author and illustrator of the Detective Penguin childrens
book series. When he was young he started drawing penguins
for the enjoyment of his brother, who loved penguins.
Now Mikel draws the penguins to bring joy to others and hopes
it helps them be as Flapping Happy as him.





The Detective Penguin Series

A series of fun and silly adventures involving the
Penguin Police Department. Join Detective Flappy,
Officer Jub-Jub, and Chief Squishy as they solve
lighthearted cases.
